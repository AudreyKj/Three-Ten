MIO ARY
A group of friends shared disposable cameras in Tokyo.

Each person took a few photographs inspired by the time 3:10 pm before passing the camera to another person.

The aim of the game was to spontaneously shoot how 3:10 pm felt like.
Concept by Mio Ary with the participation of Adam, Izumi, Kohki Hasei, Kondo, Masako, Pianola, and Tatsuhiko. ×
